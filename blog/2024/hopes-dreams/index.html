Incessant squirms of pain — a body, twisted unnaturally.
A few rays of hope, plenty for the soul to cope.
And yet unfelt energy gently guides the daily flow.

Tapestry of distasteful thoughts — a mind, trapped firmly.
A few strings of dreams, plenty to drown out the screams.
And yet undiscovered joy pierces its way through.

They will materialize — hopes & dreams.
